Click to play

Use the WASD keys to move around: W moves you forward, S moves you backward, A strafes left and D strafes right. To look around, move your mouse â€“ the game will request pointer lock when you click to begin. There is no jumping.

On touch devices, drag on the left half of the screen to move (up/down to go forward/backward and left/right to sidestep), and drag on the right half of the screen to rotate your view. Release your touch to stop moving or turning.

Press the M key at any time to toggle an overhead map of the entire maze. This lets you see where you are relative to the start (blue) and the goal (red).

Navigate through the maze and try to find your way to the glowing red goal tile!
